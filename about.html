Mandfar is a leading provider of civil engineering and mining services in Zimbabwe, specializing in large-scale projects that require precision and expertise.

Excellence Since 2019

Founded in July 2019, Mandfar Private Limited delivers professional civil and mining engineering solutions across Zimbabwe. With a mantra of continual improvement and a belief in holocracy, we empower our teams and uplift local communities. Our work aligns with Zimbabwe’s national infrastructure development goals while maintaining the highest standards as a Category A contractor with CIFOZ, the Ministry of Transport, and public works institutions. We employ over 450 staff members and actively recruit from local communities.
